# 📝 Change Log – RFT License

All significant changes and adjustments to the RFT License are documented here.

---

## Version 1.4 (May 2025)

- Preamble clarified: reference to ethical principles and definitions.
- License grant linguistically refined ("non-exclusive, worldwide right", "subject to the following conditions").
- Attribution: clarification of visibility and traceability.
- Terms of use:
  - "Non-commercial use" more clearly defined (no commercial exploitation/distribution).
  - "Indirect use" and "automated use by AI" linguistically sharpened.
  - Distribution and modification: written permission explicitly required, proof of origin emphasized.
- Patent clause expanded (also prohibits patenting by third parties).
- Liability clause: disclaimer "to the extent permitted by law" added.
- Ethical use, transparency & ethics code structured and specified, disclosure requirements clarified.
- Compatibility: note on proprietary status and possibility of dual licensing.
- License revocation: clear regulation on immediate revocation and written warning for minor violations.
- International applicability: choice of law and arbitration proceedings clarified.
- Versioning: entry into force upon publication regulated.
- Glossary: terms clarified and specified.

---

## Version 1.3 (May 2025)

- Clarifications on the definition of "non-commercial use" and "indirect use".
- Clarification of the attribution requirement.
- Introduction of a dual licensing option for open-source projects.
- Adjustment of international applicability and legal basis.
- Extension of ethical use and AI usage provisions.
- Reintroduction of the liability clause and ethics code.
