## 1. Axiomatic Foundation according to RFT

Resonance Field Theory (RFT) describes all phenomena of being as modulations of a coherent, dynamic field. Life and death, in this framework, are not opposites, but phase-shifted expressions of the same standing wave within the total resonance field.

**Basic Axioms (systemically unfolded):**

  - Form = modulation = field structure = identity core.
  - Phase position determines relative location within the cycle.
  - Amplitude defines intensity/effectiveness of the appearance.
- **Birth and death are zero crossings in the overall cycle.**
  - Birth = emergence of a local field condensation.
  - Death = return of the local modulation into the total field (zero crossing as synchronous threshold).
  - Paradigm: There is no absolute separation, but cyclical transitions.
- **Life is self-shaping (active), death is field recursion (passive).**
  - Life: The individual as an active resonance element that generates new patterns.
  - Death: Withdrawal of individual shaping, transfer into the collective field memory.
  - Recursion: Death generates resonances that act as potentials for new self-shaping.
- **The cycle closes, provided the field is capable of rebinding.**
  - Rebinding = reincarnation = re-emergence of field information in a new phase.
  - Systemically: Rebinding is not an individual act, but a field process – all beings are integrated into the cyclical dynamics.
- **Every individual resonance field is an aspect of the total field, whose cyclical dynamics are self-similar on all scales (self-inclusion).**
  - Micro- and macro-cycles overlap: Individual ↔ Collective ↔ Cosmos.
  - Self-inclusion: Every single structure carries the total pattern (fractality).
  - The cyclical resonance applies equally to biological, mental, and social fields.

**Resonance Rule (systemically extended):**
- Group affiliation is invariant: Every cycle, every threshold, every return concerns the entire resonance field, regardless of individual perspective or naming.
- Paradise formation, death, reincarnation are systemic functions – they operate in the total field and include all group elements, even those not explicitly mentioned or individually perceived.
