## 2. Yin and Yang as Resonance Phases

* **Yin** stands for the "negative" or "passive" energy phase: darkness, rest, potential, and invisibility.
* **Yang** symbolizes the "positive" or "active" energy phase: light, movement, visibility, and manifestation.

In the complete resonance field, Yin and Yang correspond to the complementary energy phases $E_-$ and $E_+$ of the energy sphere:

$$
E = E_+ + E_-, \quad \text{with } \sum_i E_i = 0
$$

This polarity is not a dichotomous separation, but a dynamic interplay ensuring the balance and completeness of the system.
