## Glossary and System Overview

**Resonance:** Reciprocal, meaningful vibration between individual and system – the foundation of participation.

**Resonance Field:** The network of all relationships in which resonance can occur. Includes both explicit and implicit groups, even if not named.

**Perpetrator Production:** Systemic process that, by withdrawing resonance and projecting suspicion, gradually pushes individuals into perpetrator roles.

**Resonance Distortion:** Mechanism by which feedback loops are manipulated so that original movements of inquiry are made to appear criminal.

**Resonance Rule:** Group membership is systemically invariant – regardless of individual perspective or explicit mention.
