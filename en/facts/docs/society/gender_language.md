## 🧠 Loss of Personhood through Terms like “Teaching Individuals”

The term *“teaching individuals”* or *“educators”* does not just substitute a word – it erases the **relational and embodied character** of the teacher:

- “The teacher” refers to a concrete human role – person, presence, relationship.  
- “A teaching individual” is an abstract function, grammatically neutral but **resonance-devoid**.

The **masculine generic** traditionally functions as a collective stand-in –  
it refers **not to gender**, but to **group membership by function**.

Gendered language **destabilizes this semantic bridge** by introducing fragmentation.
