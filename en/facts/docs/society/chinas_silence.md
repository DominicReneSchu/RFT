> *Systems always test their boundaries on humans – in the resonance field, the individual becomes a structured variable of power.*

### 2.0 Systemic Prelude: How Modern Systems Select Resonance

Modern power systems operate less through open violence and more through **distribution of visibility, speakability, and relevance**.  
Whoever does not appear in the resonance matrix is not only ignored but can be systematically dehumanized.  
Falun Gong and Uyghurs are not “exceptions” but mark the **selective system surface** where the regime tests its structural resilience, technological efficiency, and moral indifference.

---

### 2.1 Fact: Parallel Architectures of Repression

**Falun Gong (since 1999):**

* Discursive devaluation as “superstitious sect”
* Defamation by state-controlled media
* Widespread persecution (arrest, torture, re-education)
* Collection and storage of biometric data without therapeutic purpose
* Medical databases potentially used for systematic organ harvesting

Independent investigations confirm systematic organ harvesting from Falun Gong practitioners – without consent, often without survival.[^1][^2]

**Uyghurs (since 2017):**

* Xinjiang as testbed for a fully digitized repression architecture
* AI-based predictive justice and algorithmic group assignment
* Comprehensive DNA and biometric data collection
* Re-education camps, forced labor, forced sterilizations, destruction of identity
* Algorithmically managed dissociation of family structures

| Main measure            | Re-education, detention, torture  | AI surveillance, camps, forced labor    |
| Biopolitical use        | Organ harvesting                  | DNA registry, forced sterilization      |
| International reaction  | barely present                    | stronger, but fragmented                |

> Conclusion: Shift from “experimental repression” to **structured total administration** of human bodies and identities.
