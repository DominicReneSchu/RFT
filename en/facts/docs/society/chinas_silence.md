## 1. China’s Performative Silence

# Section 1: China’s Performative Silence

> *“It is not the spoken word, but consciously chosen silence that shapes the resonance field of power.”*

---

### 1.1 Fact: China’s Silence as Foreign Policy Pattern

China’s response to international crises, especially Russia’s war of aggression against Ukraine, is marked by striking restraint:

* No explicit taking of sides
* No open condemnation
* Ambivalent, formally neutral statements

This silence is no accident, but part of a strategic communication policy that uses *non-communication* as a form of influence.[^1]

---

### 1.2 Structure: Silence as Symbolic Power and System Protection

Silence is not an absence, but a performative act in the resonance field of global systems:

* **Bourdieu**: Silence as symbolic capital – it structures who *may* speak and whose voice counts.[^2]
* **Luhmann**: Non-communication creates systemic environment – silence shifts the boundaries of inclusion/exclusion.[^3]
* **Resonance Rule**: Even the unheard, unspoken, and omitted element remains part of the resonance field and feeds back systemically.

> China’s silence not only protects its own interests but shifts the global resonance point – it produces uncertainty, shapes expectations, and keeps alternative interpretations open.

---

### 1.3 Reflexive Space: Silence as Resonance Maneuver

* *Silence is never neutral*: It is a tool to assert power, sidestep responsibility, and make positions invisible while other systems are forced to act first.
* *Systemic self-inclusion*: Silence links China into the global resonance field by serving as a reference point for projections, fears, and strategic interpretations.

> In the resonance field, silence is not a deficit but the *highest form of systemic self-regulation*.

---

### 1.4 Meta-Reflection: Effects of Silence

* Every systemic silence is a resonance filter:
  It shifts not only perception but also the options for action for all involved.
* China’s performative silence can be read as a *double shield*:
  It protects its own system from exposure – and at the same time the global order from a truth that would challenge the system itself.

## 2. Falun Gong and Uyghurs as Systemic Test Groups

# 2. Human Experimentation by the System – Falun Gong and Uyghurs as Structured Resonance Experiments

> *Systems always test their boundaries on humans – in the resonance field, the individual becomes a structured variable of power.*

---

### 2.0 Systemic Prelude: How Modern Systems Select Resonance

Modern power systems operate less through open violence and more through **distribution of visibility, speakability, and relevance**.  
Whoever does not appear in the resonance matrix is not only ignored but can be systematically dehumanized.  
Falun Gong and Uyghurs are not “exceptions” but mark the **selective system surface** where the regime tests its structural resilience, technological efficiency, and moral indifference.

---

### 2.1 Fact: Parallel Architectures of Repression

**Falun Gong (since 1999):**

* Discursive devaluation as “superstitious sect”
* Defamation by state-controlled media
* Widespread persecution (arrest, torture, re-education)
* Collection and storage of biometric data without therapeutic purpose
* Medical databases potentially used for systematic organ harvesting

Independent investigations confirm systematic organ harvesting from Falun Gong practitioners – without consent, often without survival.[^1][^2]

**Uyghurs (since 2017):**

* Xinjiang as testbed for a fully digitized repression architecture
* AI-based predictive justice and algorithmic group assignment
* Comprehensive DNA and biometric data collection
* Re-education camps, forced labor, forced sterilizations, destruction of identity
* Algorithmically managed dissociation of family structures

> **Systemic Control Structure – Falun Gong vs. Uyghurs**

| Criterion               | Falun Gong                        | Uyghurs                                 |
|-------------------------|-----------------------------------|-----------------------------------------|
| Repression start        | since 1999                        | since 2017                              |
| Ideological label       | “Sect”, “Superstition”            | “Separatists”, “Religious Extremists”   |
| Main measure            | Re-education, detention, torture  | AI surveillance, camps, forced labor    |
| Biopolitical use        | Organ harvesting                  | DNA registry, forced sterilization      |
| International reaction  | barely present                    | stronger, but fragmented                |

> Conclusion: Shift from “experimental repression” to **structured total administration** of human bodies and identities.

---

### 2.2 Structure: Selective Resonance Decoupling as Technique

- Systemically, groups are defined as deviant or disruptive.
- They are deprived of resonance – dignity, voice, visibility – and decoupled from all feedback channels (cf. Luhmann: resonance through inclusion/exclusion).
- The system allows itself control, commodification, instrumentalization – up to physical eradication.
- Dignity is not only violated but **administered as a right to life** – algorithmically assigned or withdrawn (Foucault, Agamben).

> In the resonance field, human rights are not just violated, but systemically suspended – exclusion becomes a stabilizing system condition.

---

### 2.3 Reflexive Space: Systemically Administered Right to Life

- Targeted dehumanization (“sect”, “terrorist”, “disturbance”) shifts moral and legal thresholds.
- The resonance rule is perverted: Not inclusion, but exclusion becomes systemic normality.
- Human dignity mutates into an administered right to life, switchable according to political expediency.

> *A system that defines groups as disruptive perverts the resonance rule – it not only destroys their voice but undermines its own feedback ability.*

---

### 2.4 International Legal Framework and Global Complicity

- Application of medical measures without consent constitutes **torture** under Art. 1 UN Convention Against Torture.
- These practices violate the Universal Declaration of Human Rights (Art. 3, 5, 9, 18).
- International organizations (UN, Amnesty International, Human Rights Watch) document systematic human rights violations; state silence and disinformation massively block clarification.[^3][^4]
- Multinational corporations, universities, and states have cooperated for years with Chinese authorities – despite knowledge of systematic human rights abuses.[^5][^6]

## 3. Global Complicity through Economic Interdependence

# 3. Structured Complicity – How Global Markets Systemically Devalue Human Rights in the Global Resonance Field

> *In the resonance field, silence is never neutral – systems do not conceal the truth, they distribute its consequences.*

---

### 3.0 Systems Theory of Structured Complicity

Global supply chains, financial flows, and technology transfers form a tightly coupled resonance system in which responsibility is not lost but functionally distributed and shifted.  
This circular self-coupling generates stable resonance loops that mutually reinforce actions and omissions.  
The system filters moral impulses in favor of its own stability – a **resonance filtering** that creates a tension between normative claims and factual reality: **resonance dissonance**.

---

### 3.1 Fact: Beneficiaries of Systemic Obfuscation – Names, Numbers, Relations

- Nearly **45%** of global polysilicon production, a key raw material for solar modules, comes from Xinjiang (ASPI, 2020)[^1].
- Companies such as **Apple, Nike, Volkswagen, Siemens**, and **BASF** are directly or indirectly linked to forced labor in their supply chains, according to ASPI data.[^1][^2]
- Medical facilities worldwide use inexpensive organ transplants from China without making the origin and ethical screening of organs transparent.[^3][^7]
- Financial institutions invest in companies involved in surveillance, camp construction, or forced labor.
- Governments and international organizations avoid open criticism to protect geopolitical and economic interests.

This circular resonance binding mutually links beneficiaries and simultaneously restricts their room for maneuver. The geopolitical power game acts as an additional resonance context.

---

### 3.2 Structure: Resonance Deflection and Moral Disintegration

Suffering at the margins is not ignored but deliberately decoupled and redirected from its source to the center of value creation.  
The system legitimizes this disintegration as functional necessity and externalizes moral responsibility.  
This **resonance split** generates latent instability and a crisis of legitimacy, holding potential for systemic change – so long as no conscious resonance management occurs.

> *What appears as progress at the center is often merely the redistribution of suffering to the periphery.*

---

### 3.3 Reflexive Space: Media, Public Sphere, and the Production of Resonance Normality

Media and the public sphere form a societal resonance field that modulates, amplifies, or blocks resonances.  
A cumulative **resonance cascade** of spiral of silence, agenda-setting, and selective transparency systematically filters out disruptive impulses and produces social numbness, fragmentation, and loss of legitimacy.  
This creates a legitimation **resonance impoverishment**, endangering social coherence and trust.

> *What is not mirrored through resonance loses efficacy – and can be exploited without guilt.*
