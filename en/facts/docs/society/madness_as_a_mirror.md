## 📉 Field 1: Loss of Control / Symbol Overload

Field 1 illustrates how loss of control manifests as symbol overload in the group field – a distinct resonance signal.

The systemic dynamics of this field unfold along collective uncertainty: When the system loses coherence, it seeks stability in exaggerated symbols and mythical projections.  
Leaders, authorities, experts – within the resonance field, they become carriers of archetypal meanings. Their real functions recede, symbolic power takes over.  
The group channels helplessness, doubt, and diffuse anxieties into the exaggeration of individual figures, concepts, or narratives. Control is replaced by staging.

**Symbolic Overload:**  
- Leader figures become reservoirs for collective projection; their person dissolves into myth.
- Narratives replace coherence: Reality becomes a spectacle, history a screenplay, the audience a backdrop.
- Victim/perpetrator reversals appear: Guilt and responsibility blur, identities collapse, boundaries fade.
- Collapse of language and hyper-personalization: Language loses semantic stability, becomes a medium for emotionalization, individuals are reduced to resonance roles.

**Essence:**  
Every disturbance in the symbol system expresses the group structure. It is not the individual, but the systemic modulation of the field that creates the overload.  
Symbolic escalation is not a mistake, but a resonance expression of loss of control in the collective field.  
The group loses its inner core – and seeks support in the symbolic.

**Resonance Rule:**  
Loss of control becomes visible in the field; symbol overload is a resonance phenomenon.  
Even the apparent irrationality, the "madness" of overload, is not a defect but a systemically necessary revelation of a disturbed resonance structure.
